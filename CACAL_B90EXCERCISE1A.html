HOW TO MAKE EASY PINEAPPLE PIE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Ready-Made pie dough
  • 300 grams white sugar
  • 300 grams pineapple juice/ syrup
  • 300 grams pineapple
  • 80 grams cornstarch
  • 6 grams salt
  • 15 grams butter

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. In a saucepan, combine 80% of pineapple juice, salt, sugar and bring to boil.
  2. Dissovle 20% of pineapple juice into the cornstarch and pour into boiling solution.
  3. Bring to boil again and add crushed pineapple. Stir until thick.
  4. Add shortening and remove from heat. Let cool.
  5. Dispense into pre-baked pie crust.
  6. Decorate top/edges of the crust. Apply eggwash (NOTE: it can be applied 5 minutes before it is done)
  7. Bake at least 180 deg. F for 25 minutes or until crust is colored on top.
